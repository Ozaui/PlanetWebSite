 Hello little friends! Today we will explore the planet Venus. Venus is the second planet in our solar system and stands out in the sky with its bright yellow color. As it orbits the Sun, it is sometimes called the "Evening Star" and sometimes the "Morning Star". But it's too hot in here! Temperatures during the day can reach up to 450 degrees.
